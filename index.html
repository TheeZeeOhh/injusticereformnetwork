responsibilities.
As a member of the Injustice Reform Network, you are a partner in this mission.
Member rights
→ Equal share of all member profit pools from trainings and campaigns
→ Access to all training materials, legal resources, and org events
→ Voice in organizational decisions and policy work
→ Full transparency on all financial distributions
→ Future salary eligibility once the org reaches full funding
Member responsibilities
→ Actively participate in org activities and community advocacy
→ Uphold IRN values: justice, equity, transparency, accountability
→ Keep member and financial data strictly confidential
→ Contribute honestly and collaboratively to the collective mission
Member
agreement.

IRN Member Agreement
By becoming a member of the Injustice Reform Network, I commit to upholding the values of justice, equity, transparency, and community accountability. I understand the economic model and agree to participate in good faith. I will keep all member and financial data strictly confidential. Member profit distributions are in addition to any future salaries and are subject to active participation status. I commit to contributing actively to our collective mission.
